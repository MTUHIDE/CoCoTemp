External Resources

External resources that can be used with or instead of the CoCoTemp probe:

IButton

An alternative to the CoCoTemp module. Small and versatile.

IButton Adapter

An adapter to connect the iButton to a computer via USB.

GLOBE

A worldwide science and education program, providing communities the opportunity to participate in data collection and research.

CoCoRaHS

Volunteers working together to measure precipitation across the nations.

NOAA

Organization that focuses on the conditions of the oceans and the atmosphere.

NAU CoCo Temp Lab

Our partners and sponsor in creating, researching, and developing CoCoTemp.
